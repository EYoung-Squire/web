Ugly Betty

Ugly Betty is a TV show available on Hulu that follows the life of a Mexican-American woman, who suddenly got accepted for a job as the assistant in the famous magazine company "Mode".

Plot: In Ugly Betty, a Mexican-American woman called Betty Suarez lives with her sister (Hilda), nephew (Justin), and father (Ignacio) in Queens. Despite coming from a humble background, Betty has big dreams, and decides to apply for the role of assistant to the new editor-in-chief of "Mode Magazine", Daniel Meade. However, the people who work at Mode aren't exactly friendly, and she immediately struggles to be accepted in this glamorous but hostile environment. Will Betty be able to overcome the challenges that come with this job, and will she be able to make some strong friendships along the way?
